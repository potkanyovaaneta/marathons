about

This project was born from a weird obsession with marathons (and the realization that Iâ€™ll probably never run one). But I realized that race routes are actually a great way to see a ton of a city. I built this app to make the race experience accessible to everyone, transforming official marathon courses into curated "travel guides". Now, you can explore the city through the eyes of a runner, just at a much more leisurely pace.

While choosing the places I tried to make them as diverse as possible but still worth your time. I categorized them into four types - culture, nature, activities and food & drinks - based on my opinion.
